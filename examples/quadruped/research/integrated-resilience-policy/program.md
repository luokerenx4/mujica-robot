but the locked `integrated-resilience-mission` Suite alone decides promotion.

recovery, and settling. Residual authority is allowed only in locomotion after
`recoveryCompleted=true` and ramps in over 0.75 seconds. Do not remove that

It removed no locked violations. Prefer changes that improve signed
post-recovery task progress and phase-conditioned credit assignment. Record
actual Skill/Mission exposure and actor authority for every Policy.
