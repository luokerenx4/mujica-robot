# Articulated-waist design research program

Treat the waist as a bounded morphology hypothesis, not as the expected answer.

The fixed authority is the locked four-pose `self-righting` Benchmark. The editable closure is only the articulated Assembly, its Robot Base, and its matched Controller. Do not edit the rigid baseline, Task, Scenarios, Objective, Benchmark, or lock.

Each proposal must state whether it changes:

- reachable recovery workspace;
- waist roll/pitch range or leverage;
- self-contact geometry;
- leg/waist sequencing; or
- stabilization after target entry.

Judge lexicographically by enforced violations, normalized violation severity, and then score. Added mass and two extra Action coordinates remain visible design costs. Do not promote unless every recovery safety gate passes and later locomotion regressions remain intact.
