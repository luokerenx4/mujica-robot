# Spatial generalization autoresearch

Optimize a frozen residual policy across the declared delay and disturbance distribution. The held-out `spatial-generalization` Benchmark is fixed evaluation evidence and must not be weakened after a result is observed.

KEEP requires every reset, mirrored push, delay duration, and compound-disturbance gate. Aggregate score cannot compensate for survival, forward-progress, or lateral-drift failure. The 3-DOF Assembly, predictive analytic prior, task, and scenario definitions are fixed; only declared Training hyperparameters may change.
