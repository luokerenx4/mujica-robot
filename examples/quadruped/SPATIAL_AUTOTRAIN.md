# Spatial residual policy autoresearch

Train one deterministic frozen policy at a time from the promoted spatial gait prior. The 45-value Observation Contract, 12-value Action Contract, analytic prior, scenarios, task, objective, and locked Benchmark are fixed.

Change only declared Training hyperparameters. KEEP requires a higher aggregate score, every spatial gate, and no scenario-specific controller logic. A learned policy that does not beat the program controller remains evidence, not a promoted robot.
